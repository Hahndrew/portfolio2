Design process

Product Definition

Description of Process

Working in Product Management has taught me how valuable it is to clearly define the product vision and goals as they become the guide in aligning all development efforts. I see the same value applicable in the design space as it brings clarity and focus to create the right solution.

Skills & Techniques

  • Understanding business goals and objectives
  • Defining the product
  • Analytics - deriving metrics from product definition

Research & Analysis

Description of Process

I believe research & analysis provide the comprehensive understanding of the goals and needs of those who would use the software. The collective insights become the product requirements, the foundation in creating the right solution.

Skills & Techniques

  • Observation & Interview
  • Task analysis
  • Qualitative/Quantitative data analysis
  • Insights analysis & affinity diagram
  • Creating personas
  • Extracting product requirements

Usability Evaluation

Description of Process

Testing is probably the best way to validate some of the decisions made along the way. Like how the quizzes and midterms in school measure student's performance, usability test precisely reveals the weaknesses and strengths in design. Focusing on improving those weak spots can quickly enhance the usability.

Skills & Techniques

  • Write/Prepare test script
  • Facilitate test session
